Community

If you wish to contribute to the project or simply talk to us, post suggestions, feel free to reach us through:

  • IRC: join us on the channel #refuge.io on freenode.
  • Twitter: we try to answer as well on twitter from the @refugio account.
  • Google+: you can also get news and comment on our google+ page.

Mailing list

The mailing-list that can be used for support or general information about the Refuge project

  • To subscribe, send a mail to refuge+subscribe@refuge.io
  • To unsubscribe, send a mail to refuge+unsubscribe@refuge.io
  • To discuss send a mail to refuge@refuge.io
  • The web archive is available on http://lists.refuge.io/refuge
